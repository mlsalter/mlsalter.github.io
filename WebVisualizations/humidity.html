Humidity

The data shows that most of the cities around the world are above 60 % humidity. While the location, based on latitude, doesn't seem to matter there are a couple of little trends to notice. There appears to be a dip in humidity between -60 to -30 degrees and 10 to 40 degrees latitude. This would need further analysis to see if there is an actual trend or it is just based on the random cities our analysis selected.
